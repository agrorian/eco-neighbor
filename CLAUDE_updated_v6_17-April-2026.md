## 7. BUSINESS LIQUIDITY GATE — v5.0 NEW

Businesses earn ENB.GLOBAL through swap acceptance via atomic burn-and-mint on Solana.
This is NOT a payment from external fund — ENB.LOCAL metamorphoses to ENB.GLOBAL at the point of transaction.
Mint authority held by PDA smart contract only. Total supply unchanged.

**Release conditions:**
- 365-day hold | Pillar Tier (50,000 Rep Score) | Active acceptance required at release
- Max 50,000 ENB per release event (governance vote above this)
- Max 2 releases per year, 6-month minimum gap between them
- **No lifetime cap** (unlike Maturation Bridge — sustained participation is rewarded without ceiling)
- Disclosure in monthly redemption log

Rep Points earned on every swap — businesses can reach Pillar Tier through volume.
